About the Project

Breast Cancer Diagnosis Model using Mammography Images by Employing Deep Learning


Inspiration

As breast cancer is the second common cause of death resulting from cancer in women, breast cancer is a serious health threat to women, both physically and psychologically. Mammography is an inexpensive method where one can diagnose breast cancer in its early stages.

Mammography images can show changes in the breast up to two years before a physician can feel them. Computer-aided detection and diagnosis are considered to be one of the most promising approaches that may improve the efficiency of mammography.


Aspiration

The aim of this project is to design a high accuracy breast cancer diagnosis model using deep learning approaches to
classify the cancerous and non-cancerous cases of mammography images.


The Team

We are a group of 3 final year computer science students majoring in data science developing this predictive model
as part of our final year project at Monash University, Malaysia.

Our team hopes that this project will have a substantial impact towards the field of breast cancer detection.
